Simple website for creating your own quizzes.

This is the place where you can create your own “which character are you?” quizzes

Instruction
Fill in all the required fields and click Create/Update button to create or update your character options.
Click Done button to proceed to next page in order to take your new created quiz.
Click Reset button to reset all information that you've just typed in the required fields.

The character of a quiz taker is determined by the answer option that is chosen the most. For example, if a quiz taker create 3 questions, 2/3 answers are optionA. Then, characterA will be chosen as the character of quiz taker
